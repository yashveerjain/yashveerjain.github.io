About Me

I am currently pursuing a master's in Robotics from University of Maryland College Park, with a background is in computer vision and machine learning and I did my bachelor's in technology in Mechatronics from Manipal University Jaipur. I have also worked as a research intern for 4 months in swaayatt Robotics ( company which builds ADAS software) where I worked on computer vision and researching its application in self driving cars. So I always thrive to work on such problems where any if such field is included.
